TF2 Stats

2021

A website to view your personal Team Fortress 2 Stats.

NodeJS, Javascript, HTML5, CSS3

GitHub Repo

Website


I made this after finding out nothing similar already existed.

Users just need to put in their username, and all of their information will automatically be fetched from the Steam Web API.

This was made using NodeJS and Express to handle requests, and is currently hosted on Heroku.
