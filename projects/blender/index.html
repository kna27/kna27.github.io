Money

Download

Stacks of all of the USD bills! The currency strap is the correct color for each bill type. Random rotation and transformation has been applied to each bill to make them look more varied. A normal map has also been used to make the bills look worn out and as if they had been touched and handled. All materials for the bills and the currency straps are procedural. Inspiration and help for the idea came from a Default Cube video.
